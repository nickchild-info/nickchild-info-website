The Challenge

Clinical teams relied on a patchwork of spreadsheets, manual SQL exports, and inconsistent metric definitions. Data refreshes were slow, audits were painful, and conflicting numbers undermined trust.

Core Pain Points

Single-source-of-truth issues, delayed reporting cycles, and limited access to governed datasets.

The Solution

We consolidated data into a centralized warehouse, standardized transformation logic, and introduced semantic models for consistent metrics across teams.

Platform Blueprint

Ingestion Modernization

Replaced ad-hoc extracts with scheduled pipelines and automated data validation checks.

Metric Governance

Defined shared metric definitions with clinical leads and embedded them in dbt models.

Self-Serve Analytics

Delivered curated data marts and Looker explores to reduce analyst bottlenecks.

Snowflake dbt Airflow Looker

Impact

Leadership gained reliable dashboards for operational planning while analysts reclaimed time previously spent on manual data prep.
